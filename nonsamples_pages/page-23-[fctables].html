Our ranking calculates in real time, and shows the classification to be published on 3 october 2019. You can simply check that you national team has been promoted or dropped from a particular place. Our ranking is unique, because it presents the current total score of all the teams before the official, monthly ranking announcement by the football federation. You do not have to refresh the page if the game changes the score, the ranking will refresh the points and position of the team itself.

    What is a FIFA ranking?
    Our website we presents the prediction of FIFA world rankings that will be published next month. Our ranking we generate in real time. As soon as the goal is scored in any team match, the table will be automatically refreshed. Over the list we present the time of the last update.

    The official national teams ranking is available on fifa.com. In FcTables, with our database, you can check predicted position your national team during, or just after the match. We calculate the ranking in the same way FIFA does.
